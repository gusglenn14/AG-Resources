Our Leadership

Meet the experienced professionals driving innovation in agricultural energy

FOUNDER AND PRESIDENT

Gavin Glenn

From 2018-2025 Gavin served as President of Americlean Energy Group. Gavin's responsibilities included preparing wind leases, reviewing all the contracts for Americlean for a 400 MW wind farm located in Woodford County, Illinois. Additionally, Americlean developed two projects totaling 750 MW of power in Callahan County, Texas.

Prior to his renewable energy endeavors, Gavin served as an in-hous attorney for several small to large companies focusing on the transactional side of oil, gas and renewable energy law for various exploration and production and mineral purchasing clients This includes drafting and reviewing purchase and sale agreements, title reports, land management projects.

Gavin graduated from Southern Methodist University with a B.S. in in Mechanical Engineering in 1999 and a juris doctorate from Texas Tech University in 2005.

Our Track Record
